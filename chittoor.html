Chittoor District Tourism

Explore Chittoor—the green hills of Horsley, divine shrines of Kanipakam & Srikalahasti, serene falls, and thriving wildlife sanctuaries await!

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

One of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, located in Tirupati.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Tirupati
  • Best Time: September–February
  • Activities: Temple visit, hill trekking
Horsley Hills

Known as the 'Ooty of Andhra Pradesh', this hill station offers cool climes, scenic views, and dense forests.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Oct–Mar
  • Activities: Trekking, safari, nature walks
Kanipakam Vinayaka Temple

An 11th-century Dravidian-style Ganesha temple, famed for its self-manifested idol that grows over time.

Travel Tips:
  • Location: ~11 km from Chittoor
  • Festivals: 21-day Brahmotsavams in August–Sept
Srikalahasti Temple

A revered Shiva temple famous for Vayu Linga and its breath-taking gopurams and carvings.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Pilgrimage, architecture, rituals
Sri Venkateswara National Park

A biosphere rich in wildlife—elephants, leopards, giant squirrels, and rare gliding lizards thrive here.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Nov–Feb
  • Activities: Wildlife safaris, nature trails
Nagalapuram Waterfalls

A picturesque trekking route takes you through forested trails to series of gentle falls.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Monsoon to early winter
  • Activities: Trekking, photography, dip in falls
