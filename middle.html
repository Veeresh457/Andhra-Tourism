Middle Andhra Tourism

Middle Andhra is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic temples, coastal beauty, and bustling cities like Vijayawada, Guntur, and Ongole. Explore spiritual, natural, and cultural attractions of the region.

Kanaka Durga Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga, located on the Indrakeeladri Hill overlooking the Krishna River in Vijayawada.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Temple visit, Krishna river boating
Bhavani Island

One of the largest river islands in India, offering boating, water sports, and beautiful picnic spots on the Krishna River.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Boating, water sports, picnics
Undavalli Caves

Ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 4th–5th century, known for their magnificent architecture and sculptures.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: October–February
  • Activities: Sightseeing, photography
Chirala Beach

A peaceful beach in Prakasam district, ideal for relaxation and enjoying the scenic coastal beauty of Andhra Pradesh.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Chirala
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Beach walk, relaxation
Amaravati Stupa

An ancient Buddhist monument and heritage site, significant for its archaeological importance and spiritual heritage.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Guntur
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Historical exploration, photography
