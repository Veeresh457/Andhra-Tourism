Vizianagaram District Tourism

Vizianagaram district, the "City of Victory," is known for its rich cultural heritage, palaces, temples, and scenic natural beauty.

Vizianagaram Fort

A historical fort built in the 18th century by Maharaja Vijaya Rama Raju, showcasing the glory of Vizianagaram Samsthanam.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vizianagaram
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Heritage walk, photography
Ganta Stambham (Clock Tower)

A landmark structure in Vizianagaram town built during the British era, symbolizing the district's heritage.

Travel Tips:
  • Located in: Vizianagaram Town
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Sightseeing, photography
Thatipudi Reservoir

A scenic reservoir surrounded by hills, ideal for picnics, boating, and enjoying nature.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Vizianagaram (20 km)
  • Best Time: July–February
  • Activities: Boating, nature walks, photography
Ramateertham

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Rama, located on a hillock, with Buddhist and Jain relics nearby.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Vizianagaram (12 km)
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Temple visit, trekking, sightseeing
Bobbili Fort

Built in the 18th century, this fort is famous for the Bobbili battle and the valour of its rulers.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Bobbili (55 km from Vizianagaram)
  • Best Time: October–February
  • Activities: Historical exploration, photography
