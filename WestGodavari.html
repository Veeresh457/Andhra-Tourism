West Godavari District Tourism

West Godavari, known for its coconut groves, canals, temples, and cultural heritage, is a serene destination for nature and culture lovers.

Dwaraka Tirumala Temple

A famous hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, known as Chinna Tirupati.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Eluru
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Pilgrimage, sightseeing, photography
Kolleru Lake

One of India’s largest freshwater lakes, famous for bird watching and serene landscapes.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Eluru
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Birdwatching, boating, photography
Guntupalli Caves

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with monasteries, dating back to 3rd century BC.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Eluru
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Historical exploration, photography, learning history
Pedavegi Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Chaturbhujeswara, rich in cultural heritage.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Eluru
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Pilgrimage, sightseeing, photography
Eluru Canal

A scenic canal passing through lush green landscapes, ideal for peaceful evening walks.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Eluru
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Walking, photography, boat rides
