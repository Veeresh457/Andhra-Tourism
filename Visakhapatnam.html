Visakhapatnam District Tourism

Visakhapatnam, known as the "City of Destiny," is famous for beaches, hills, temples, and cultural heritage.

RK Beach

A popular beach for sunrise views, leisure walks, and cultural events in Vizag.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Visakhapatnam
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Beach walk, photography, local food
Kailasagiri Hill Park

Hilltop park with giant statues, ropeway, and panoramic views of the city and sea.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Visakhapatnam
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Ropeway ride, sightseeing, photography
Araku Valley

Scenic hill station with coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture experiences.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Visakhapatnam (120 km)
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Trekking, nature walks, sightseeing
Borra Caves

Limestone caves with stalactites and stalagmites, a natural wonder of the Eastern Ghats.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Araku (90 km)
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Cave exploration, photography
Simhachalam Temple

Famous temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha, noted for architectural beauty and festivals.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Visakhapatnam
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Temple visit, spiritual rituals
Submarine Museum

INS Kursura, a decommissioned submarine now open as a museum showcasing naval history.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Visakhapatnam
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Museum visit, photography, naval history
