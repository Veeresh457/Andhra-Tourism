South Andhra Tourism

South Andhra Pradesh is known for its historical temples, scenic beaches, and the famous Tirumala Hills. Explore the spiritual and natural beauty of the region.

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

One of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, located in Tirupati.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Tirupati
  • Best Time: September–February
  • Activities: Temple visit, hill trekking
Srikalahasti Temple

A renowned temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, famous for its intricate architecture and religious importance.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Tirupati
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Spiritual visit, photography
Pulicat Lake

India’s second largest brackish water lake, known for its bird sanctuary and scenic beauty.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Sullurpet
  • Best Time: November–January
  • Activities: Bird watching, boating
Nellore Beach

A serene beach in Nellore district, perfect for a peaceful evening by the Bay of Bengal.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Nellore
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Beach walk, relaxation
Talakona Waterfalls

The highest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh, located in the Sri Venkateswara National Park.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Tirupati
  • Best Time: August–January
  • Activities: Trekking, photography, nature walks
