Krishna District Tourism

Krishna District, with Vijayawada as its heart, is famous for Kanaka Durga Temple, Prakasam Barrage, Kondapalli Fort, beaches, and cultural richness.

Kanaka Durga Temple

One of the most famous temples in Andhra Pradesh, located on Indrakeeladri hill overlooking the Krishna River.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: Year-round (especially during Dasara)
  • Activities: Spiritual visit, rituals, photography
Prakasam Barrage

A beautiful barrage across Krishna River, offering stunning evening views and vibrant city lights.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Evening walks, photography, local food nearby
Kondapalli Fort

A historic fort surrounded by hills, known for its strategic importance and Kondapalli toy craft village nearby.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Kondapalli (25 km from Vijayawada)
  • Best Time: October–February
  • Activities: Heritage walk, trekking, toy shopping
Manginapudi Beach

A scenic beach near Machilipatnam, famous for its natural black soil and peaceful environment.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Machilipatnam
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Beach walk, photography, seafood tasting
Bhavani Island

A beautiful river island on Krishna River near Vijayawada, offering boating, water sports, and nature stays.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Boating, water sports, camping
Undavalli Caves

Ancient rock-cut caves near Vijayawada, famous for their huge monolithic statue of Lord Vishnu.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Vijayawada
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Heritage exploration, photography, study of architecture
