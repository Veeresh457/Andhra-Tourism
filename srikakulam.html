Srikakulam District Tourism

Srikakulam district, known as the "Andhra Kashmir," is famous for its scenic beaches, temples, and ancient Buddhist heritage sites.

Kalingapatnam Beach

A serene beach famous for its lighthouse, ancient port history, and peaceful sunsets.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Srikakulam (26 km)
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Relaxation, photography
Arasavalli Sun Temple

One of the few Sun temples in India, dedicated to Lord Surya, believed to have been built in the 7th century.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Srikakulam (2 km)
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Temple visit, spiritual rituals
Srikurmam Temple

The only temple in India dedicated to Lord Vishnu in the form of a tortoise (Kurma Avatar).

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Gara (15 km from Srikakulam)
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Temple darshan, festivals
Salihundam Buddhist Site

An ancient Buddhist site with stupas and sculptures located on the banks of the River Vamsadhara.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Gara (15 km)
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Heritage walk, archaeology tours
Baruva Beach

Known as the "second Goa of Andhra," this beach has a historical port and picturesque surroundings.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Sompeta (27 km)
  • Best Time: October–January
  • Activities: Relaxation, picnics, beach walk
