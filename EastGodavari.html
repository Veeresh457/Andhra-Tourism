East Godavari District Tourism

East Godavari, famous for its lush green paddy fields, Godavari River, temples, and cultural heritage, is a nature lover’s paradise.

Dowleswaram Barrage

A historic barrage built across the Godavari River, perfect for evening walks and photography.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Rajahmundry
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Sightseeing, photography, boating nearby
Kotilingeswara Temple

A famous Shiva temple with a thousand lingas, attracting devotees from across the state.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Rajahmundry
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Spiritual visit, rituals, photography
Kadiyam Botanical Gardens

Famous for exotic plant species and lush greenery, ideal for nature walks and relaxation.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Kadiyam (10 km from Rajahmundry)
  • Best Time: October–February
  • Activities: Nature walks, photography, picnics
Papi Hills

A scenic river hill range along the Godavari, perfect for boating, trekking, and nature sightseeing.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Rajahmundry
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Boating, trekking, photography
ISKCON Temple

Modern temple known for its spiritual ambiance and vibrant festivals in Rajahmundry.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Rajahmundry
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Spiritual visit, cultural events, photography
Rajahmundry River

A peaceful beach along the Godavari riverfront, ideal for evening strolls and sunset photography.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Rajahmundry
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Beach walk, photography, local food sampling
