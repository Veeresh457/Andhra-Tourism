North Andhra Tourism

North Andhra is famous for its pristine beaches, lush green hills, ancient temples, and vibrant culture. Explore the gems of Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram, and Srikakulam districts.

Rama Krishna Beach (RK Beach)

A popular beach in Visakhapatnam, perfect for morning walks, sunsets, and leisure activities. Close to museums and parks.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Airport: Visakhapatnam Airport (12 km)
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Beach walk, photography, water sports
Araku Valley

A scenic hill station known for coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture. Surrounded by Eastern Ghats mountains.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Railway: Araku Railway Station
  • Best Time: September–February
  • Activities: Trekking, coffee museum visit, tribal dance
Borra Caves

Majestic limestone caves with stalactites and stalagmites, located amidst lush green hills and waterfalls.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Araku (35 km)
  • Best Time: October–January
  • Activities: Cave exploration, photography
Kalingapatnam Beach

A serene beach in Srikakulam district, famous for its lighthouse, history, and peaceful sunsets.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest Town: Srikakulam (26 km)
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Relaxation, photography
Simhachalam Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Varaha Narasimha, blending architectural beauty with spiritual significance.

Travel Tips:
  • Nearest City: Visakhapatnam (20 km)
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Temple visit, spiritual rituals
