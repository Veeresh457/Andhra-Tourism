Nellore District Tourism

Explore Nellore—where ancient temples, serene beaches, rich wildlife, and vibrant history meet along the Penna and Bay of Bengal.

Sri Talpagiri Ranganathaswamy Temple

An ancient Vishnu temple (12th century) on the banks of Penna River, adorned by a grand Gaaligopuram.

Travel Tips:
  • Location: Nellore
  • Best Time: March–April for Brahmotsavam
  • Activities: Worship, heritage photography
Penchalakona Temple

A peaceful Narasimha shrine near a scenic waterfall, perfect for devotion and nature walks.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Pilgrimage, easy trekking, nature
Mypadu Beach

Golden sands and calm waves on the Bay of Bengal, with growing attractions like water sports and resorts.

Travel Tips:
  • Location: ~25 km from Nellore
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Beach walks, boating, sunset watching
Nelapattu Bird Sanctuary

A birdwatcher's delight—home to pelicans, flamingos, storks, and over 180 species of migratory birds.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: November–February
  • Activities: Birdwatching, photography, tranquil trail walks
Udayagiri Fort

Historic hill fort offering panoramic views, with layers of Chola, Vijayanagara and local cultural heritage.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: October–March
  • Activities: Heritage walk, panoramic photos, light trekking
Narasimha Konda

A 350-ft hill near Jonnawada with an ancient Narasimha shrine, built by Pallava king Narasimhavarman I.

Travel Tips:
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Activities: Pilgrimage, light trek, panoramic hilltop views
